Martabak

Ingredients:

  • 250 grams of all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 300 ml water
  • 2 stalks of green onions (thinly sliced)
  • Oil as needed

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Mix the flour, egg, salt, and water. Stir until well combined.
  2. Add the green onions and mix again.
  3. Heat oil in a pan.
  4. Pour the batter into the pan and spread evenly.
  5. Cook until both sides are done and golden brown.
  6. Remove and serve while warm.
