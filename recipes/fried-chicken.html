Fried Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 500 grams of chicken
  • 3 cloves of garlic (ground)
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for frying

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Grind the garlic, coriander, and salt.
  2. Coat the chicken with the ground spices.
  3. Add a little water and simmer the chicken until tender.
  4. Heat enough oil in a pan.
  5. Fry the chicken until golden brown.
  6. Remove and drain before serving.
