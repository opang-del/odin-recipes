Omelette

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 stalk of green onion (thinly sliced)
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl.
  2. Add the green onion, salt, and pepper.
  3. Beat until all ingredients are well combined.
  4. Heat oil in a pan.
  5. Pour in the eggs and cook until done on both sides.
  6. Remove and serve.
